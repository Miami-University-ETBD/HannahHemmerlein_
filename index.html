About Us

Heritage Crafts has been serving the greater New York City area since 1999. We promise legacy in each piece, which is handcrafted with love and care. The sky is the limit when it comes to our pottery and our vision. Our team of talented artists is dedicated to creating special pieces that can bring a unique sense of style and joy to your home and your everyday life.

Our Products

At Heritage Crafts, we specialize in a variety of handcrafted, one-of-a-kind ceramic products. Our signature items include:

  • Colorful dinnerware perfect for any table
  • Unique vases and crocks
  • The perfect mugs to start your morning
  • Dog bowls for your furry friends
  • Decor and other custom items for the home
  • And so much more!

Each item is crafted carefully by hand. Whether you're looking for a special gift or a new addition to your daily routine, we have something for your every need.
